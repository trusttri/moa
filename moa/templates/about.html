Moa is how you pronounce "모아" — which roughly means "gather" in Korean.

Moa is a system for consentfully connecting PhD students affected by faculty advisors’ mistreatment or abuse of power.

Moa lets you have full control over who can see what you share -- based on your consent boundaries. First set your consent boundary settings in this page.
